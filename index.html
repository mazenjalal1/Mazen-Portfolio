The Beginning

I joined Yemen's first internet-based media company after participating in almost all of the school's art activities. I was the youngest show presenter in Yemen at the time. Aside from the work I was assigned, I helped with a variety of projects, including filmmaking and photography. The experiences allowed me to meet and work with talented individuals, propelling me further into the world of media production.

My role in the production: Actor + Host

Yemenis in Exile Podcast

A podcast where we share stories of Diaspora Yemenis, sharing their experiences about conflicts in Yemen and how they struggled before, during, and after the war, until they went abroad.
The project is part of the Digital Steps Program , specifically tailored to the Yemenis Diaspora, organized in collaboration between BuildUp and The British Council.

My role in the production:

  • Transcription of the Audio recordings.
  • Organizing the script and flow of the story.
  • Editing all the episodes.
  • Alongside my colleague, recording with guests.
